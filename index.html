I am a second year phD candidate at Nanyang Technological University, Singapore studying under Dr. Quang-Cuong Pham (CRI group). My research interests align with motion planning and control for robotics. Our works can be found in the research page.

Currently I am looking at possible applications of Reinforcement Learning to robotics. Despite the recent surge in popularity of Reinforcement Learning, there has yet been any convincing proof showing that these techniques are competitive for robotic tasks with stringent safety requirements.
